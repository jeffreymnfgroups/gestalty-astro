subtitle: The System
title: What Is the Learning Acumen System?
description: The Learning Acumen System is a high-order learning behavior tracker designed to measure, reinforce, and elevate learning quality by focusing not just on what is studied, but how it is learned, practiced, and retained.

what_it_does_title: What It Does
what_it_does_list:
  - "**Bringing awareness to blind spots** – See what you can't see on your own"
  - "**Encouraging deliberate learning** – Focus on quality over quantity"
  - "**Promoting intrinsic excellence** – Build habits that drive mastery"
  - "**Converting abstract traits into data** – Turn critical thinking, discipline, and metacognition into trackable weekly behaviors"

result_title: The Result
result_description: Objective clarity on what's working and what needs correction—transforming effort into direction, and direction into predictable progress.
result_list:
  - Align learners with higher-order cognitive goals
  - Support self-regulated learning (SRL) and deliberate practice
  - Promote deep work, not just volume of study
